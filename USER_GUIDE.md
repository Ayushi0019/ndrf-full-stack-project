# NDRF Disaster Management Portal - Operational Manual

This guide provides the core steps for managing a real-time disaster response mission.

## 1. Visual Intelligence (The Map)
*   **Blue Pins**: Represent **Rescue Units** (Teams, Boats, Helicopters). Click them to see current status.
*   **Red Pulses**: Represent **Disaster Hotspots**. These are the areas where response is currently focused.

## 2. Strategic Setup (HQ Admin)
*   **Create a Disaster**: 
    1.  Click the **"Report Disaster"** button in the top right of the HQ Dashboard.
    2.  Fill in the name (e.g., "Assam Floods") and location details. 
    3.  This places the red impact zone on the map.
*   **Register Assets**:
    1.  Click **"New Registration"** to add a logic entry for a new team, vehicle, or equipment set.
    2.  Specify their starting location and type.

## 3. Mission Execution (HQ Admin)
*   **How to Send "XYZ" Team to a Disaster**:
    1.  Find the team in the **"Operational Assets"** list.
    2.  Notice the **Paper Plane Icon** (Dispatch) on their row.
    3.  Click this icon. Their status changes to **"Active"**, transmitting their deployment status throughout the network.
*   **AI-Assisted Dispatch**:
    1.  Look at the **"AI-Suggested Dispatch"** card on the right-hand side.
    2.  The system identifies the 3 closest standby units to the active disaster.
    3.  **Simply click any suggested unit** to mobilize them immediately.

## 4. Ground Reporting (Field Member)
*   **Self-Assignment**: Use the **"Self-Assign Unit"** button if you are not yet assigned to a team.
*   **Proof of Site**: Go to the **"Proof"** tab to upload situational photos. These appear in the HQ "Ground Proof" gallery instantly for visual verification.
*   **Resource Requests**: Use the **(+)** button in the "Needs" tab to ask for supplies from HQ.
